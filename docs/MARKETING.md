# Marketing & Growth Strategy - Stamplicity

## 30-Day Launch Plan ("Go-to-Market")

### Week 1: Foundation & Alpha
- **Day 1-3**: Finalize PWA and mobile builds. Ensure SEO optimization in `index.html`.
- **Day 4-7**: Internal testing and bug fixes. Set up GitHub Pages landing page.

### Week 2: Social Proof & Beta
- **Day 8-10**: Launch on Product Hunt (Upcoming) and Reddit (r/stamps, r/philately).
- **Day 11-14**: Invite 50 power users for closed beta. Gather testimonials.

### Week 3: Paid Acquisition (Free Credits)
- **Day 15-21**: Deploy "Free Credit" campaigns:
  - **Google Ads**: Utilize $500 free credit for "stamp valuation" and "identify stamp" keywords.
  - **Meta/Instagram**: $50-100 credit for visual ads targeting collectors.
  - **GitHub Sponsors**: Outreach to open source philately projects.

### Week 4: Public Launch
- **Day 22-25**: Submit to App Store (via Capacitor) and Google Play.
- **Day 26-30**: Public launch event. High-volume social media posting.

## 90-Day Forecast
- **Month 2**: Reach 5,000 active sessions. 2% conversion to Premium ($500 MRR).
- **Month 3**: Partnership with 1 major philately blog. Reach 15,000 active sessions. 3% conversion ($1,500+ MRR).
